FifaWorldCup-2018 instalation process on Android Device;


1. Open Google Play Store.
2. Click on Search Bar.
3. Type FifaWorldCup-2018 and Search.
4. Select The FifaWorldCup-2018 App.
5. Click on Install.
6. Accept the license agreement.
7. After installation, click the Open button to use the app .

Note: Before accept the agreement read the agreement very carefullys
